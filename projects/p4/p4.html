[P-04] Jericho Model Rocket

This is the Jericho model rocket. I was a part of the team that made it. It is probably the first of its kind - a rocket made on discord. The discord server named "Rocketry IN" was the team behind this. They seem to have spun off this whole model rocket making into a venture by the name of Othisis Systems Pvt. Ltd. I do not have much to do with the company, but was quite active back when this was just called Rocketry IN and more focused on student science projects. The company is pretty cool focusing on making India's first fully reusable rocket.

I was involved with making CAD models for the rocket. Specifically the linear actuator used for parachute recovery.
